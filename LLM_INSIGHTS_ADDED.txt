HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

1. USER SUBMITS ASSESSMENT
   -----------------------
   Patient fills out the risk assessment form with 17 clinical features

2. AI PREDICTION
   -------------
   Data-driven model analyzes risk factors and generates prediction

3. LLM INSIGHTS GENERATION
   -----------------------
   LLMInsightsGenerator analyzes:
   - Patient data
   - Prediction results
   - Risk factors
   - Health status
   
   Generates:
   - Executive summary
   - Risk analysis
   - Personalized recommendations
   - Medical considerations
   - Follow-up plan

4. COMPREHENSIVE RESULTS
   ---------------------
   User receives:
   - Risk prediction
   - Probability score
   - Detailed AI insights
   - Actionable recommendations
   - Educational resources
